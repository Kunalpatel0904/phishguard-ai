# phishguard-ai
PhishGuard AI is a web-based tool powered by Artificial Intelligence that instantly detects phishing emails, fake job offers, scholarship scams, and social engineering attacks. Users paste any suspicious text or message into the app, and PhishGuard AI returns a clear verdict — DANGER, WARNING, or SAFE — along with a risk score (0-100%), a breakdown of detected red flags, and clear action steps on what to do next. The tool is designed specifically for students and everyday users who lack technical knowledge to identify cyber threats on their own. No login required, no installation, works instantly in any browser.
